Golden-Doodles

Fun Facts about Golden-Doodles

Goldendoodles are friendly, loving, and sociable. They are also known for being hypoallergenic.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Goldendoodles are a crossbreed of a Golden Retriever and a Poodle. They are known for their intelligence, loyalty, and playfulness. Goldendoodles are a good choice for first-time dog owners because they are easy to train and eager to please. They are also good with children and other pets. However, because they are so friendly and loyal, they are prone to separation anxiety, especially as puppies.

Teacup Bichon Frises

Fun Facts about Teacup Bichon Frises

They are energetic but don't require much exercise. They are hypoallergenic. They are adaptable and sociable. They don't drool and don't shed much. They can live in an apartment or a country home. They can be difficult to house train.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Bichon Frises are descended from water spaniels. They are 9.5 to 11.5 inches tall at the withers and weigh 12 to 18 pounds. They have a short blunt muzzle, silky ears, and a puffy, silky, curled coat.

Wolf Dog

Wolf dogs don't bark, they howl. While some breeds of dogs also howl, most bark. Wolf dogs with more than 80% wolf DNA are more likely to resemble wolves in appearance and behavior. At 95%, they are almost indistinguishable from wolves. Wolf dogs are escape artists and can jump over 8' fences, dig holes under fences, and open doors and gate latches.
